Overview

Purpose

The purpose of The Rafting Club is to provide a new way of choosing the way to spend the holidays, both as a family and individually, proposing activities such as Rafting, kayaking, hiking and camping as they allow us to renew energy, the Rafting trips are marked by a type of clear water and a specific place that allows you not only to enjoy this activity but also to appreciate the beautiful natural landscapes, living new quality moments with family and friends is ideal for people who want to disconnect from their work from the life and have a weekend or a good family vacation, we also have a large staff of teachers and people specialized in the health sector, we are also working to include new family activities.

Audience

Rafting Club is aimed at people who like to create unique and unforgettable moments, who like to spend time alone, with friends and families, it is a place designed for all types of ages starting from 16 to 60 years old, we will also allow them to participate in the programs schools that want to do recreation in nature, each instructor must have a group in charge of a maximum of 15 people who through a mobile or tablet application will be connected to be able to register their locations in real time

Wireframes

Wireframes are like blueprints for making webpages. They should show the major sections of content that will be on the page and the relative locations of each element. In the wireframe below you can see there will be 6 sections to our page:

  1. At the top we have a section with the logo and the navigation bar.
  2. Then there is a banner image that stretches all the way across the screen.
  3. Next we have some text and an image
  4. ...followed by another row made up of an image and some text.
  5. Then one more section of text with no image.
  6. Lastly, a footer containing a copyright/name line and 3 social media icons.
